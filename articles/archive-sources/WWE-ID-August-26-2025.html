WWE Independent Development™ (WWE ID) is designed to provide independent wrestlers a clear pathway to a potential career in WWE. 

Following the success of WWE's NIL program, WWE ID has been constructed to support independent wrestling prospects and schools with world-class training, development and mentorship, in turn raising the profile of and strengthening the independent wrestling ecosystem. 

WWE will provide the most prominent independent wrestling schools in the U.S. with the "WWE ID" official designation, with the goal of guiding new trainees and existing talent at these select institutions with enhanced developmental opportunities. 

WWE ID will identify the top independent wrestling prospects with an official "WWE ID Prospect" designation and support their developmental journey. WWE ID will give fans the opportunity to follow the paths of these standout prospects on the independent wrestling scene through curated, behind-the-scenes content, as well as highlights and matches showcased across WWE's social platforms.

WWE ID SCHOOLS
REALITY OF WRESTLING

Reality Of Wrestling is a top-tier professional wresting school that trains & showcases unsigned talent from around the world. Established in 2005, Reality Of Wrestling is owned and operated by Booker T & Sharmell Huffman where they provide professional wrestlers the opportunity to develop their skills to take their careers to the next level.

Elite Pro Wrestling Training Center

The Elite Pro Wrestling Training Center is the top destination for aspiring wrestlers in the Northeast! Led by coaches Mike Hollow and Scott Reed, who bring 50+ years of experience from top professional wrestling organizations around the world. Our comprehensive training curriculum ensures you're well-equipped for a successful career!

Learn more
Nightmare Factory

Nightmare Factory, founded in 2020, is a Professional Wrestling Camp led by Undisputed WWE Champion, Cody Rhodes! This beginner camp is designed to teach the basics needed to pursue the ultimate dream, to become a WWE Superstar.  Athletes have the chance to study film, learn the art of the promo along with a required reading of “A Challenge For The Actor” by Uta Hagen, provided during camp as well as having access to our private, fully equipped gym for strength and conditioning training.  Guest Coaches such as WWE Superstars John Cena, Sheamus & countless others have also shared their knowledge to the athletes!

Black & Brave Wrestling Academy

Located in Davenport, Iowa, the Black & Brave Wrestling Academy is run by legendary WWE superstar Seth Rollins and independent wrestling veteran Marek Brave. Since opening in 2014, BxB has been a premiere destination for aspiring wrestlers across the globe. 

Providing elite in-ring training, character work and development, fitness classes and more, BxB is the perfect facility for experienced wrestlers and beginners alike.
